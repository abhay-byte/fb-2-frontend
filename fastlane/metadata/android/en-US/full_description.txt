Finalbenchmark 2 is a comprehensive CPU benchmarking tool designed to measure your Android device's processing performance with precision and accuracy.

<b>Key Features:</b>
• Multi-threaded CPU stress testing with real-time monitoring
• Single-core and multi-core performance benchmarks
• Temperature tracking during intensive tests
• No internet connection required
• Completely free with no ads or tracking

<b>Why Benchmark Your CPU?</b>
Understanding your device's CPU performance helps you make informed decisions about app development, troubleshoot performance issues, and compare different Android devices before purchase. Whether you're a developer, enthusiast, or simply curious about your device's capabilities, Finalbenchmark 2 provides the data you need.

<b>How It Works:</b>
The app runs a series of mathematical calculations, cryptographic operations, and multi-threaded workloads to push your CPU to its limits. Tests include floating-point calculations, integer operations, compression algorithms, and stress testing to measure both peak performance and sustained throughput under load.

<b>Privacy & Security:</b>
Finalbenchmark 2 runs completely offline - no data is sent to external servers. All benchmark results are stored locally on your device, ensuring complete privacy and security of your performance data.
